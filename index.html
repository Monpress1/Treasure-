1. Scope of Agreement

This document constitutes the official Software Update and Maintenance Agreement between Monpress Web Solutions (MWS) ("Developer") and Belgom Network ("Client") regarding the continued development and maintenance of the BELGOM Progressive Web App (PWA).

This contract operates strictly on a "Pay-Per-Update" model. MWS is retained solely for executing specific, requested technical interventions, code improvements, and feature deployments on the Belgom platform.

2. Communication & Delivery Timelines

To ensure rapid response times and clear execution, the following protocols apply:

  • Strictly WhatsApp: All project communication, update requests, approvals, and delivery notices will be conducted exclusively via WhatsApp. Email communications (such as Gmail) or other chat platforms are not recognized for official updates.
  • Delivery Timeline: Once an update is finalized and payment terms are met, the completed fixes or features are expected to be deployed and fully functional within 3 days.

3. Categories of Fixes & Pricing Structure

Prices for updates are not fixed; they are discussed and agreed upon via WhatsApp based strictly on the complexity and level of the fix required. The categories are defined as follows:

  • Minor Fixes: Small adjustments such as text modifications, basic UI alignment, or color changes.
  • Standard Fixes: Integration of single APIs, addition of new pages, or functional bug resolutions within existing logic.
  • Major Fixes: Core system architecture changes, heavy database modifications, or the deployment of entirely new complex modules.

4. Payment Terms

To commence any work on the BELGOM platform, the following strict payment rules apply:

  • Advance Payment Rule: For all Minor and Standard fixes, payments must be made in full before any development or fixes commence.
  • Major Fixes Structure: For Major fixes, a down payment is required to initiate the 3-day delivery timeline. The remaining balance is strictly due upon the successful completion of the major fix.
  • No revisions or additional features will be accepted as part of a pending update until the prior update is fully cleared and paid.
